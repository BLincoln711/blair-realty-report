What We Implemented

1 AI-Optimized Content Strategy

Created cornerstone content designed for AI comprehension and citation. Built the "Top Home Builders in Cypress TX 2025 Guide" as a pillar page with clear H2/H3 structure, FAQ sections, and definitive expertise statements that AI systems can extract and cite.

Result: +2,260% impressions on guide page

2 Geographic Content Expansion

Built neighborhood-specific pages targeting submarkets: Clay Residential, Houston metro areas, and surrounding communities with localized content.

Result: /neighborhoods/ +267%

3 Agent Authority Building

Optimized individual agent pages with structured bios, expertise statements, and local market credentials that establish each agent as an entity.

Result: Agent pages +335% avg

4 Schema Markup Implementation

Added FAQPage, LocalBusiness, Article, and BreadcrumbList schema across key pages to help AI systems understand and cite content.

Result: Enhanced search features

5 Content Cluster Architecture

Connected supporting pages (mentorship, podcast, agent recruitment) back to cornerstone content, establishing topical authority signals.

Result: /mentorship/ +231%

6 Search Console Optimization

Weekly validation of indexing status, fixed "discovered not indexed" issues, and requested re-indexing after content optimizations.

Result: 1,353 keywords ranking
