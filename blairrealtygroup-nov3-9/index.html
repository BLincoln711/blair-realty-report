Campaign Analysis:

  • Consumer Brand Awareness: Lower 0.47% CTR indicates room for creative refresh and targeting refinement to improve engagement.

Recommended Actions:

  1. Review campaign settings and daily budgets ($4/day each) to ensure optimal configuration
  2. Evaluate quality scores and ad relevance to maximize impression share
  3. Explore targeting expansion opportunities to reach more qualified audiences
  4. Test new ad creative variations, particularly for the consumer campaign to improve CTR
  5. Consider shifting more budget to the agent campaign given its stronger 10% CTR performance
  6. Enhance conversion tracking to better measure campaign effectiveness and ROI

Observation: Average engagement time decreased 31% from 46 seconds to 32 seconds this week. This provides an opportunity to enhance user experience and content engagement.

Recommendation: Conduct a technical and UX audit focusing on: page load speeds, mobile experience quality, content presentation, and navigation flow. Review Google Analytics behavior patterns to understand user preferences. Consider implementing heat mapping tools to identify engagement opportunities and optimize high-traffic pages.

This dual presence means Blair Realty Group owns the entire conversation before prospects even consider competitors.

Recommendation: Replicate this total search strategy across other high-value keywords. Create comprehensive, authoritative content that can capture both AI Overview features and top organic rankings.
